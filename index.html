Artist Statement

Why she keeps drawing dragons instead of going to bed on time.

Rachael Greenfield

Fantasy illustrator & character artist

Rachael is obsessed with stories. Every drawing starts with a “who are they?” and “what kind of trouble are they in?” From quiet hedge witches to over-dramatic dragon riders, she uses shape, costume, and expression to make characters feel like people you could actually adventure with.

Available for book covers, TTRPG parties, and character commissions.
Comfortable working from written prompts, vibes, or detailed references.
Delivers high-resolution files ready for print or digital use.

Approach. Most pieces start as small, messy thumbnails in a sketchbook. Once the pose and silhouette feel right, Rachael refines line work and then builds up lighting and color to match the mood of the story.

Subjects. Player characters, original fantasy worlds, cozy magic, and “I swear this NPC matters” side characters. She loves drawing expressive faces, layered outfits, and environments that hint at a larger world.

Materials. Pencil and ink on paper, marker rendering, and digital painting in Procreate / Photoshop for final pieces and client work.
